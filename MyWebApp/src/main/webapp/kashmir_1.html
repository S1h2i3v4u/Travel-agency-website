Detailed Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Srinagar

Arrive at Srinagar Airport and transfer to your houseboat on Dal Lake. Enjoy a relaxing Shikara ride in the evening.

Activities:

  • Airport transfer to houseboat
  • Welcome drink on arrival
  • Evening Shikara ride (1 hour)
  • Dinner on houseboat

Day 2: Srinagar Sightseeing

Full day tour of Srinagar including Mughal Gardens and local markets.

Activities:

  • Visit Nishat Bagh (The Garden of Pleasure)
  • Explore Shalimar Bagh (The Garden of Love)
  • Tour of Pari Mahal (The Fairies' Abode)
  • Local market shopping

Day 3: Gulmarg Excursion

Day trip to Gulmarg with Gondola ride to Kongdoori Mountain.

Activities:

  • Scenic drive to Gulmarg (2 hours)
  • Gondola cable car ride (Phase 1 & 2)
  • Free time for optional activities
  • Return to Srinagar in evening

Day 4: Pahalgam Tour

Full day excursion to Pahalgam valley with visits to scenic spots.

Activities:

  • Drive to Pahalgam (3 hours)
  • Visit Betaab Valley and Aru Valley
  • Lunch at local restaurant
  • Optional pony ride (at own expense)

Day 5: Sonmarg Excursion

Day trip to Sonmarg, the "Meadow of Gold".

Activities:

  • Scenic drive to Sonmarg (2.5 hours)
  • Visit Thajiwas Glacier (optional sledding)
  • Picnic lunch by Sindh River
  • Return to Srinagar in evening

Day 6: Departure

Transfer to airport for your onward journey.

Activities:

  • Breakfast at houseboat
  • Check-out and transfer to airport
  • Depart with unforgettable memories

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Kashmir?

The best time to visit Kashmir is from March to October when the weather is pleasant. Spring (March-May) offers beautiful blossoms, summer (June-August) is ideal for sightseeing, and autumn (September-October) has stunning fall colors. Winter (November-February) is perfect for snow activities.

What should I pack for this trip?

For summer months, pack light woolens for evenings and comfortable clothing for daytime. For winter, bring heavy woolens, thermal wear, gloves, and a good jacket. Regardless of season, bring comfortable walking shoes, sunglasses, sunscreen, and any personal medications.

Is this package suitable for elderly travelers?

Yes, this package is suitable for elderly travelers with moderate mobility. The itinerary can be customized to reduce physical exertion. Please inform us in advance so we can arrange for special assistance if needed.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 30 days prior to departure receive 90% refund, 15-30 days prior receive 50% refund, and less than 15 days prior are non-refundable. We recommend purchasing travel insurance to cover unforeseen circumstances.

Are there any special requirements for visiting Kashmir?

Indian citizens don't require any special permits for this itinerary. Foreign nationals should check visa requirements. All travelers should carry valid photo ID (Aadhar/Passport). Some areas may require additional permits which we can arrange if needed.
